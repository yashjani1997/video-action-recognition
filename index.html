// 06 — Limitations

Known Limitations

Trained on 5 classes only — UCF-101 has 101 total action categories
Small dataset subset — performance may vary on unseen action types
Fixed 16-frame window may miss context in longer action sequences
CricketShot & TennisSwing share motion overlap — harder to distinguish
